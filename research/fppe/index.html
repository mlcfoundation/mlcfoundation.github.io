Family Planning Performance Explorer

The Family Planning Performance Explorer provides estimates of 21 family planning performance indicators for India and for its constituent states/Union Territories and districts as derived from the data available from the National Family Health Survey 2015-2016 and 2019-2021. The explorer covers the folowing aspects of family planning performance: 1. Family planning methods prevalence 2. Satisfied demand of modern family planning methods 3. Unmet need of family planning 4. Family planning method skew 5. Family planning performance index 1. 1.
